Speed Pool





Welcome to Speed Pool almost exactly like regular Pool except the color or pattern has no importance only the time it takes you to pot all the balls Key differences are: If you POT the CueBall you LOSE immediately Game Over and have to fully restart If the cue ball is stationary you can still hit it before waiting for all the balls to become stationary rewarding accurate play



To send the cue ball you click anywhere within the area of the given image; Marked by the X in the image The ball will go in that direction in which you clicked with a certain velocity; Marked by the arrow Finally the speed of the ball is measured by how far away you clicked from the cue ball The greater the distance away the greater the speed

Good Luck and Have fun
